STARTER GUIDE

Melon is easy to use and could generate melodies for any tastes! Follow our 5-step guide and get started right now!

STEP 1

 

Register/login to the system.

For new users, try the "Create your account" section. For existing users, please log in directly.

 

STEP 2

You can choose to change your profile to let us better know you!

Then click "Generate Music" to create some melody you like!

​

STEP 3

Before generating melody, please choose your favorite lyrics to fit!

We give you some recommendations on the left and lyrics searching tools on the right. Feel free to try it!

 

After selecting your favorite lyrics, fill them in the textarea and click "Confirm your lyrics" and "Next Step"!

 

STEP 4

On the next page, you can choose the key and instrument to fit with the melody. After selection, please click "Generate"!

STEP 5

In this page, you can listen to the melody generated with the lyrics.

We also have voice recorder tool under the music player. Try to click the "Record" button and sing along with the melody!

After recording, please click the "Combine" button. Then we will combine two tracks and show you the final music!

This is your own work and feel free to download it and share with your friends!

Quite simple isn't it! Now you can try it by yourself!
